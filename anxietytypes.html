Agoraphobia

What is Agoraphobia?

Agoraphobia is the intense fear of situations and/or places that might cause emotions such as embarrasement, panic, or helplessness.


Symptoms of Agoraphobia

There are many different symptoms but a few are included below.
• Being in open spaces
• Using public transportation
• Being in enclosed spaces
• Being outside of the home alone
• Standing in line or being in a crowd


Anxiety disorder due to a medical condition

What is Anxiety disorder due to a medical condition?

Anxiety disorder due to a medical condition is the symptoms of severe anxiety that is directly caused by physical health problem.


Symptoms of Anxiety disorder due to a medical condition

Although this form of Anxiety isn't purely based off symptoms, there are some that can be included.
• Muscle tension
• Restlessness
• Sweating
• Nausea
• Chest pains


Generalised Anxiety Disorder

What is Generalised Anxiety Disorder?

Generalised Anxiety Disorder (GAD) is characterised by ongoing anxiety that interfers with everyday life.


Symptoms of Generalised Anxiety Disorder

Generalised Anxiety Disorder symptoms can range bahavioural symptoms to physical and psychological, including: • Restlessness
• Sweating
• Trouble sleeping
• Difficulty concentrating
• Overthinking


Panic Disorder

What is Panic Disorder?

Panic Disorder is characterised by the frequent accurance of panic attacks.


Symptoms of Panic Disorder

Panic Disorder is mainly characterised by the frequent accurance of panic attacks, and the signs of this disorder includes:
• Sweating
• Chest pain
• Racing heart
• Trembling
• Feeling out of control


Seperation Anxiety

What is Seperation Anxiety?

Seperation Anxiety is commonly known as the childhood disorder of intense fear of being seperated from loved ones.


Symptoms of Seperation Anxiety

Symptoms of Seperation Anxiety are often quite distinguishable, being obvious signs such as:
• Refusing to sleep alone
• Reluctant to be alone
• Clingy behaviour
• Panic at times of seperation
• Worry about being parted from family members


Social Anxiety

What is Social Anxiety?

Social Anxiety is the persistent fear of being watched and/or judged by others.


Symptoms of Social Anxiety

People with Social Anxiety disorder often display physical symptoms of awkwardness and/or nervousness.
• Racing heart
• Stomach ache
• Blushing
• Soft voice
• Sweating
