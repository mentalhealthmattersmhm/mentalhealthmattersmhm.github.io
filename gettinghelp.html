Sometimes professional support is needed in a short amount of time. In these situations, phone calls to online services are usually the best option. Some of these include:

Emergency Services

In case of an emergency, emergency services are available. In the case of mental health, call in the case that you are about to hurt yourself or others.
000
24/7

Suicide Call Back Service

National services that provides free 24/7 phone, video and online professional counselling to people who are affected by suicide.
1300 659 467
24/7

Lifeline

24-hour crisis support telephone service. Lifeline provides 24/7 crisis support and suicide prevention services.
13 11 14
24/7

QLife LGBTI counselling

QLife phoneline and web chat provides anonymous and free LGBTIQ peer support and referral for people want to talk about sexuality, identity, gender, bodies, feelings or relationships.
1800 184 527
3pm to Midnight

NSW Mental Health Line

A mental health professional will answer your call about mental health concerns for you or someone you are concerned about.
1800 011 511
24/7

Kids Helpline

A telephone counselling support line for children and young individual between 5 and 25 years of age.
1800 55 1800
24/7

Beyond Blue

You can speak with a trained mental health professional any time of the day. You will be listened to, given information and advice, and directed in the right direction for further assistance.
1300 22 4636
24/7

Young Carers Helpline

Gives young people tips on how to look after themselves and the person they are caring for.
1800 242 636
24/7

MensLine

MensLine Australia is a telephone and online counselling service offering support for Australian men anywhere, anytime.
1300 78 99 78
24/7

Mindspot

MindSpot is a free service for Australian adults who are experiencing difficulties with anxiety, stress, depression and low mood.
1800 61 44 34
Open Monday to Friday 8 am to 8pm, Saturday 8am to 6pm

Butterfly Foundation

Online and phone counselling, information, and online support groups for people concerned about eating disorders, disordered eating, body image problems.
1800 334 673
8am – Midnight, 7 days a week
